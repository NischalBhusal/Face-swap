Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FaceSwap.

Is it really free?
Yes, completely free. There's no subscription, no credits, and no hidden fees. The app is built entirely on open-source models (InsightFace + GFPGAN) and you can self-host it for free.
Do you store my photos?
No. Your images are written to temporary files during processing and immediately deleted after the swap is returned. Nothing is stored on disk or logged beyond server error messages.
What image formats are supported?
JPEG, PNG, and WebP are accepted. Maximum file size is 10 MB per image. The result is always returned as a JPEG.
Why does it sometimes fail?
The model requires a clearly visible, reasonably front-facing face to detect. Sunglasses, extreme angles, heavy blur, or very small faces may cause detection to fail. Try a clearer, better-lit photo.
Can I swap multiple faces?
Yes — the app swaps the source face onto every detected face in the target image automatically. Group photos work well as long as faces are clearly visible.
